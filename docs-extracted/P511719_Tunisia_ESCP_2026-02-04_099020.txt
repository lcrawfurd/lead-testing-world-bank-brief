INDICATORS FOR IMPLEMENTATION READINESS
The following actions are indicators for implementation readiness:
                1. Recruitment of environmental, social, and OHS specialists and appointment of local E&S focal points and confirm their assignment within
                   the PMU (A).
                2. ESMF prepared and disclosed: the ESMF (including screening tools, exclusion list, and site-specific ESMP requirements) is prepared, consulted, and
                     disclosed (1.2)
                3.   Capacity building for the E&S Specialists of the PMU and the local focal points (A)
                4.   Detail and develop E&S staffing and assignments at the central, and local levels in the project operational manual (POM)
                5.   Prepare and validate the Labor Management Procedures (LMP) (2.1).
                6.   Implementation of the grievance mechanism (GM) for workers (2.2).
                7.   Preparation and validation of the SEA/SH action plan for this project (n 4.1).
                8.   Put in place a fully operational grievance mechanism, including a specific process for handling SEA/SH-related incidents for this project
                     (10.2).
                9. Stakeholder Engagement Plan (SEP) prepared and disclosed: the SEP is prepared, consulted, disclosed, and the Project GM is established and operational
                     (10.1 , 10.2).
                10. Integration of all relevant E&S requirements into bidding documents for works, goods, and supervision contracts (ESMPs, LMP, OHS, SEA/SH,
                    GM, Site-Specific ESMP obligations) prior to procurement launch (Action 1.2).
                11. Operationalization of incident /accident notification procedures aligned with ESIRT requirements, including internal reporting lines and
                    responsibilities (Action E).
