             ENVIRONMENTAL AND SOCIAL COMMITMENT PLAN

1.   The Socialist Republic of Viet Nam (the Borrower) will implement the Mekong Delta
     Climate Resilience and Integrated Transformation Project (the Project), with the involvement
     of the Ministry of Agriculture and Environment (MAE), its Central Project Office (CPO) and
     Infrastructure Construction Management Board No. 10 (ICMB 10), and the Provincial/City
     People’s Committees of Tay Ninh, Vinh Long, Dong Thap Provinces and Can Tho City, as
     set out in the Loan Agreement and the Grant Agreement (collectively, the Agreements). The
     International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), acting in its own
     capacity and as administrator of the trust fund administered by the Bank, has agreed to
     provide financing for the Project as set out in the Agreements.
2.   The Borrower, through MAE, shall ensure that the Project is carried out in accordance with the
     Environmental and Social Standards (ESSs) and this Environmental and Social Commitment
     Plan (ESCP), in a manner acceptable to the Bank. The ESCP is a part of the Agreements. Unless
     otherwise defined in this ESCP, capitalized terms used in this ESCP have the meanings ascribed
     to them in the Agreements, as relevant.
3.   Without limitation to the foregoing, this ESCP sets out material measures and actions that the
     Borrower, through MAE, shall carry out or cause to be carried out, including, as applicable,
     their respective timeframes; institutional, staffing, training, monitoring and reporting
     arrangements; and grievance management. The ESCP also sets out the environmental and
     social (E&S) documents that shall be prepared or updated, consulted, disclosed and
     implemented under the Project, consistent with the ESSs, in form and substance acceptable to
     the Bank. Said E&S documents may be revised from time to time with prior written agreement
     by the Bank. As provided for under the referred Agreements, the Borrower, through MAE,
     shall ensure that there are sufficient funds available to cover the costs of implementing the
     ESCP.
4.   As agreed by the Bank and the Borrower, this ESCP will be revised from time to time, if
     necessary, to reflect adaptive management of Project changes or unforeseen circumstances or
     in response to Project performance. In such circumstances, the Bank and the Borrower, through
     MAE, agree to update the ESCP to reflect these changes through an exchange of letters signed
     between the Bank and the Borrower, through MAE. The Borrower, through MAE, shall
     promptly disclose the updated ESCP.
5.   The subsection on “Indicators for Implementation Readiness” below identifies the actions and
     measures to be monitored to assess Project readiness to begin implementation in accordance
     with this ESCP. Nevertheless, all actions and measures in this ESCP shall be implemented as
     set out in the “Timeframe” column below irrespective of whether they are listed in the referred
     subsection.
