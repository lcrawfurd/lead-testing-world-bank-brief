1. The Republic of Mali (the Beneficiary) will implement the Water Security Support Project in Mali (PASEMa) in
   association with the Ministry of Water (SOMAPEP SA, SOMAGEP SA and DNH) and the Ministry of Economy
   and Finance, as indicated in the Financing Agreement. The International Development Association (the
   Association) has agreed to provide financing for the Project as set out in the relevant agreement(s).

2. The Beneficiary shall ensure that the Project is carried out in accordance with the Environmental and Social
   Standards (ESS) and the provisions of this Environmental and Social Commitment Plan (ESCP), in a manner
   acceptable to the Association. The ESCP is part of the Financing Agreement. Unless otherwise specified in this
   ESCP, capitalized terms used herein have the meanings ascribed to them in the applicable agreement(s).

3. Without prejudice to the foregoing provisions, this ESCP sets out the concrete measures and actions that the
   Beneficiary will implement or ensure are implemented, including, where applicable, the deadlines for these
   actions and measures, the institutional framework, staffing, training, monitoring, and reporting mechanisms
   and the complaints management mechanism. The ESCP also defines the environmental and social instruments
   that must be adopted and implemented within the framework of the Project, be the subject of prior
   consultations and be made public, in accordance with the NES, and in a manner deemed acceptable, in form
   and substance, by the Association. Once adopted, the said environmental and social instruments may be
   revised from time to time with the prior written consent of the Association.

4. As agreed by the Association and the Republic of Mali, this ESCP may be revised from time to time during the
   implementation of the Project, if necessary, in a manner that takes into account the adaptive management of
   changes or unforeseen situations that may arise within the framework of the Project, or in response to an
   evaluation of the performance of the Project. In such situations, the Beneficiary through the Ministry
   responsible for Water and the Association agree to revise the ESCP accordingly, by an exchange of letters
   signed between the Association and the Beneficiary. The Beneficiary will publish the revised ESCP without
   delay.

        survivor-centered manner.
