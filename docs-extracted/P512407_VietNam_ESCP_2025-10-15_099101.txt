             ENVIRONMENTAL AND SOCIAL COMMITMENT PLAN

1.   The Socialist Republic of Viet Nam (the Borrower) will implement the Sustainable Fisheries
     Development Project (the Project), with the involvement of the following agencies: the Ministry
     of Agriculture and Environment (MAE) through the Agricultural Project Management Board
     (APMB); and the Borrower’s Hai Phong City, Thanh Hoa Province, Gia Lai Province, Khanh Hoa
     Province, and An Giang Province (collectively, Project Provinces and Cities), as each of them is
     represented by its respective people’s committee (collectively, C/PPCs); as set out in the Loan
     Agreement for the Project – Ministry of Agriculture and Environment (the Agreement). The
     International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) has agreed to provide
     financing for the activities under the Project to be carried out by MAE in the Project Provinces
     and Cities, as described in Schedule 1 to the Agreement (MAE’s Project).
2.   The Borrower, through MAE, shall, and shall cause the Project Provinces and Cities to, ensure that
     the MAE’s Project is carried out in accordance with the Environmental and Social Standards (ESSs)
     and this Environmental and Social Commitment Plan (ESCP), in a manner acceptable to the Bank.
     The ESCP is a part of the Agreement. Unless otherwise defined in this ESCP, capitalized terms
     used in this ESCP have the meanings ascribed to them in the Agreement.
3.   Without limitation to the foregoing, this ESCP sets out material measures and actions that the
     Borrower, through MAE, shall, and shall cause the Project Provinces and Cities to, carry out or
     cause to be carried out, including, as applicable, their respective timeframes; institutional, staffing,
     training, monitoring and reporting arrangements; and grievance management. The ESCP also sets
     out the environmental and social (E&S) documents that shall be prepared or updated, consulted,
     disclosed and implemented under the MAE’s Project, consistent with the ESSs, in form and
     substance acceptable to the Bank. Said E&S documents may be revised from time to time with
     prior written agreement by the Bank. As provided for under the referred Agreement, the Borrower,
     through MAE, shall, and shall cause the Project Provinces and Cities to, ensure that there are
     sufficient funds available to cover the costs of implementing the ESCP.
4.   As agreed by the Bank and the Borrower, this ESCP will be revised from time to time, if necessary,
     to reflect adaptive management of MAE’s Project changes or unforeseen circumstances or in
     response to MAE’s Project performance. In such circumstances, the Bank and the Borrower,
     through MAE, agree to update the ESCP to reflect these changes through an exchange of letters
     signed between the Bank and the Borrower, through the MAE. The Borrower, through MAE, shall
     promptly disclose the updated ESCP.
5.   The subsection on “Indicators for Implementation Readiness” below identifies the actions and
     measures to be monitored to assess MAE’s Project readiness to begin implementation in
     accordance with this ESCP. Nevertheless, all actions and measures in this ESCP shall be
     implemented as set out in the “Timeframe” column below irrespective of whether they are listed
     in the referred subsection.

     Agreement. Unless otherwise defined in this ESCP, capitalized terms used in this ESCP have the

     the referred Agreement, the Borrower, through Ca Mau PPC, shall ensure that there are sufficient
     funds available to cover the costs of implementing the ESCP.

   Agreement (Can Tho City’s Project).

   in accordance with the Environmental and Social Standards (ESSs) and this Environmental and
   Social Commitment Plan (ESCP), in a manner acceptable to the Bank. The ESCP is a part of the
   Agreement. Unless otherwise defined in this ESCP, capitalized terms used in this ESCP have the
   meanings ascribed to them in the Agreement.
3. Without limitation to the foregoing, this ESCP sets out material measures and actions that the
   Borrower, through Can Tho CPC, shall carry out or cause to be carried out, including, as applicable,
   their respective timeframes; institutional, staffing, training, monitoring and reporting arrangements;
   and grievance management. The ESCP also sets out the environmental and social (E&S) documents
   that shall be prepared or updated, consulted, disclosed and implemented under the Can Tho City’s
   Project, consistent with the ESSs, in form and substance acceptable to the Bank. Said E&S
   documents may be revised from time to time with prior written agreement by the Bank. As provided
   under the referred Agreement, the Borrower, through Can Tho CPC, shall ensure that there are
   sufficient funds available to cover the costs of implementing the ESCP.
4. As agreed by the Bank and the Borrower, this ESCP will be revised from time to time, if necessary,
   to reflect adaptive management of Can Tho City’s Project changes or unforeseen circumstances or
   in response to Can Tho City’s Project performance. In such circumstances, the Bank and the
   Borrower, through Can Tho CPC, agree to update the ESCP to reflect these changes through an
   exchange of letters signed between the Bank and the Borrower through Can Tho CPC. The Borrower,
   through Can Tho CPC, shall promptly disclose the updated ESCP.

   measures to be monitored to assess Can Tho City’s Project readiness to begin implementation in
   accordance with this ESCP. Nevertheless, all actions and measures in this ESCP shall be
   implemented as set out in the “Timeframe” column below irrespective of whether they are listed in
   the referred subsection.

1.   The Socialist Republic of Viet Nam (the Borrower) will implement the Sustainable Fisheries
     Development Project (the Project), with the involvement of the Thanh Hoa Provincial People’s
     Committee (Thanh Hoa PPC) through the Provincial Project Management Unit (PPMU) in Thanh
     Hoa Province, as set out in the Loan Agreement for the Project – Thanh Hoa Province (the
     Agreement). The International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) has agreed
     to provide financing the activities under the Project to be carried out by Thanh Hoa PPC in Thanh
     Hoa Province, as described in Schedule 1 to the Agreement (Thanh Hoa Province’s Project).
2.   The Borrower, through the Thanh Hoa PPC, shall ensure that the Thanh Hoa Province’s Project is
     carried out in accordance with the Environmental and Social Standards (ESSs) and this
     Environmental and Social Commitment Plan (ESCP), in a manner acceptable to the Bank. The
     ESCP is a part of the Agreement. Unless otherwise defined in this ESCP, capitalized terms used
     in this ESCP have the meanings ascribed to them in the Agreement.
3.   Without limitation to the foregoing, this ESCP sets out material measures and actions that the
     Borrower, through Thanh Hoa PPC, shall carry out or cause to be carried out, including, as
     applicable, their respective timeframes; institutional, staffing, training, monitoring and reporting
     arrangements; and grievance management. The ESCP also sets out the environmental and social
     (E&S) documents that shall be prepared or updated, consulted, disclosed and implemented under
     the Thanh Hoa Province’s Project, consistent with the ESSs, in form and substance acceptable to
     the Bank. Said E&S documents may be revised from time to time with prior written agreement by
     the Bank. As provided for under the referred Agreement, the Borrower, through Thanh Hoa PPC,
     shall ensure that there are sufficient funds available to cover the costs of implementing the ESCP.
4.   As agreed by the Bank and the Borrower, this ESCP will be revised from time to time, if necessary,
     to reflect adaptive management of Thanh Hoa Province’s Project changes or unforeseen
     circumstances or in response to Thanh Hoa Province’s Project performance. In such circumstances,
     the Bank and the Borrower, through the Thanh Hoa PPC, agree to update the ESCP to reflect these
     changes through an exchange of letters signed between the Bank and the Borrower through Thanh
     Hoa PPC. The Borrower, through Thanh Hoa PPC, shall promptly disclose the updated ESCP.
5.   The subsection on “Indicators for Implementation Readiness” below identifies the actions and
     measures to be monitored to assess Thanh Hoa Province’s Project readiness to begin
     implementation in accordance with this ESCP. Nevertheless, all actions and measures in this ESCP
     shall be implemented as set out in the “Timeframe” column below irrespective of whether they are
     listed in the referred subsection.
